About the Game

Distribution Simulator 2025 is a business simulation game that puts you in control of a packaging distribution company. With strategic and operational challenges, you'll need to make careful decisions to ensure the success of your business.

Explore different aspects of business management, from delivery logistics to negotiation with suppliers and marketing strategies. Face unexpected events that will test your management skills in diverse situations.

Get ready for an immersive experience where every decision will impact the growth and success of your company.
