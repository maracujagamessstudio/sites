About the Game

Cardboard Tower is a fun game where you need to build a tower using cardboard boxes. Explore different types of boxes and settings as you construct the tallest tower possible. Test your skill and balance to prevent the tower from collapsing! With captivating graphics and simple mechanics, Cardboard Tower offers a relaxing and challenging experience for all ages.
